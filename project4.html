Tombow MONO Poster

This was a straightforward project for a course where we were asked to create an informative poster for an existing product.

At the time, my laptop froze and I couldn't use Photoshop, so I turned to Google Drawings instead. While not a professional platform, it allowed me to focus on basic design and layout, which were the most important aspects of the assignment.

I used the masking tool on my iPhone to remove backgrounds from photos I took myself, and did my best to make Google Drawings work. Given the tools and time I had, Iâ€™m proud of the final result.

You might notice a reference to Jeremy Allen White. Early in the semester, my group decided to include him or one of his projects in every submission. It started as a joke but turned into a creative challenge that pushed us to think more critically and make our work more engaging.
