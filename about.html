About Me

I’m Zoe Cooper, a sophomore at CU Boulder majoring in Creative Technology & Design—an engineering degree with a focus on design and human interaction—and minoring in Engineering Entrepreneurship. I’m enthusiastic about creating all kinds of art, especially writing music and crafting. I firmly believe that art is whatever you make it and that it holds a meaningful place in the world. Through my work, I strive to highlight the complexity and wonder of the world, encouraging others to engage with it through creativity and shared knowledge.
